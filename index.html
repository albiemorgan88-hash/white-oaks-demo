Recovery Resources

🛡️ Coping with Triggers

Strategies to handle difficult moments

  • HALT - Am I Hungry, Angry, Lonely, or Tired?
  • Call your sponsor or support person
  • Go to a meeting (online or in-person)
  • Practice deep breathing: 4 counts in, hold 4, out 4
  • Remove yourself from the triggering situation
  • Remember: This feeling will pass

🌿 Grounding Exercises

Techniques to stay present and calm

  • 5-4-3-2-1: 5 things you see, 4 you hear, 3 you touch, 2 you smell, 1 you taste
  • Feel your feet on the ground
  • Name 5 things in the room around you
  • Hold an ice cube or splash cold water on your face
  • Count backwards from 100 by 7s

🙏 The Serenity Prayer

A cornerstone of recovery wisdom

"God, grant me the serenity to accept the things I cannot change,
the courage to change the things I can,
and the wisdom to know the difference."

🪜 The 12 Steps

Foundation of your recovery journey

A gentle reminder of your foundation. Discuss with your sponsor.

  1. Admitted we were powerless...
  2. Came to believe in a Power greater than ourselves...
  3. Made a decision to turn our will and lives over...
  4. Made a fearless moral inventory...
  5. Admitted the exact nature of our wrongs...
  6. Were entirely ready to have God remove defects...
  7. Humbly asked Him to remove our shortcomings...
  8. Made a list of all persons we had harmed...
  9. Made direct amends wherever possible...
  10. Continued to take personal inventory...
  11. Through prayer and meditation improved conscious contact...
  12. Having had a spiritual awakening, we tried to carry this message...
